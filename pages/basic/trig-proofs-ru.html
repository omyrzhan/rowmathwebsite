Доказательства всех формул

Назад к формулам

6 аксиом:
1. sin(a+b) = sin a cos b + cos a sin b
2. cos(a+b) = cos a cos b − sin a sin b
3. sin(a−b) = sin a cos b − cos a sin b
4. cos(a−b) = cos a cos b + sin a sin b
5. sin²a + cos²a = 1
6. sin 30° = 1/2

tan θ = sin θ / cos θ

tan θ = sin θ / cos θ (по определению)

cot θ = cos θ / sin θ

cot θ = 1 / tan θ = cos θ / sin θ

1 + tan²θ = sec²θ

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1 → делим на cos²θ:
tan²θ + 1 = sec²θ

1 + cot²θ = csc²θ

Делим на sin²θ → 1 + cot²θ = csc²θ

sin(−θ) = −sin θ

sin(−θ) = sin(0 − θ) = −sin θ

sin 2θ = 2 sin θ cos θ

sin(θ+θ) = 2 sin θ cos θ

sin a + sin b = 2 sin((a+b)/2) cos((a−b)/2)

sin a + sin b = sin x + sin y, где x = (a+b)/2 + (a−b)/2, y = (a+b)/2 − (a−b)/2
= 2 sin((a+b)/2) cos((a−b)/2)

sin a sin b = (1/2)[cos(a−b) − cos(a+b)]

cos(a+b) + cos(a−b) = 2 cos a cos b
cos(a+b) − cos(a−b) = −2 sin a sin b
→ sin a sin b = (1/2)[cos(a−b) − cos(a+b)]

cos a cos b = (1/2)[cos(a−b) + cos(a+b)]

cos(a+b) + cos(a−b) = 2 cos a cos b
→ cos a cos b = (1/2)[cos(a−b) + cos(a+b)]

sin a cos b = (1/2)[sin(a+b) + sin(a−b)]

sin(a+b) + sin(a−b) = 2 sin a cos b
→ sin a cos b = (1/2)[sin(a+b) + sin(a−b)]

cos a sin b = (1/2)[sin(a+b) − sin(a−b)]

sin(a+b) − sin(a−b) = 2 cos a sin b
→ cos a sin b = (1/2)[sin(a+b) − sin(a−b)]

cot(α ± β) = (cot α cot β ∓ 1) / (cot β ± cot α)

cot(α + β) = cos(α+β) / sin(α+β)
Делим на sin α sin β → (cot α cot β − 1) / (cot α + cot β)

cos 30° = √3/2

sin²30° + cos²30° = 1 → cos 30° = √3/2
